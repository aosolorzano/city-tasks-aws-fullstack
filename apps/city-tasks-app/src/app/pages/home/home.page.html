City Tasks Service

Welcome to our innovative full-stack application, meticulously architected for native deployment on Amazon Web Services (AWS). Crafted with the sophisticated Ionic Framework, Angular, and Amplify, our application offers a seamless experience for managing and orchestrating Linux-like CRON jobs directly from AWS's powerful backend.

This application is powered by a robust Spring Boot Native microservice that harnesses the capabilities of the Quartz library, facilitating the precise scheduling of recurring tasks. The microservice is elegantly deployed on AWS Fargate ECS, utilizing the AWS Copilot tool for an optimized cloud-native experience.

Our platform is strategically designed to synergize cron timer execution with an array of AWS services. It provides the agility to automate operations for devices at pre-set times and specific days, bringing precision and reliability to your scheduling needs. The application is especially beneficial for Internet of Things (IoT) projects, integrating effortlessly with AWS IoT Core, Lambda functions, DynamoDB, and Cognito for a comprehensive and secure IoT ecosystem.

Leverage our application to elevate your IoT solutions, streamline operations, and harness the full potential of cloud automation with AWS.
